# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want SACA to be a respectful, constructive, and inclusive project for
contributors, reviewers, researchers, and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in discussions.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Share context and validation when proposing changes.
- Treat safety-related concerns seriously and respond conservatively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language.
- Intentionally unsafe guidance for medical or research workflows.
- Publishing private credentials, personal information, or internal-only data.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions, comments, or issues that
violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you see conduct issues, report them privately to the maintainers through the
repository contact path or security workflow.
